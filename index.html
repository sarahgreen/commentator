Welcome to the Commentator App!

Use this app to get comments from articles on the New York Times Online.

Getting started:

Click "Comments from a day" to get comments posted on a certain day.

Click "Comments from an article" to get a set of comments on a certain article.

Tips:

Click a commenter's name to view more comments by that person.

Click the stars to rate a comment.

Click the "Comment leaderboard" link in the bottom left corner to view a table of comments you've rated. Click the arrows in the column headers to sort comments by rating or author.

Click the buttons at any time to retrieve more comments.

Other questions or problems? Click the "Need help?" link in the bottom right corner.

Use the buttons at the top of the page to retrieve comments posted on articles on the New York Times Online.

Click "Comments from a day" to get comments posted on a certain day. Then enter a date in the box that pops up, using YYYYMMDD (YearMonthDay) format.

Click "Comments from an article" to get a set of comments on a certain article.

Click a commenter's name to retrieve more comments by that person.

Click the link below "Original Article" in a comment block to see the article on which that comment was posted.

The article will be displayed in a new window.

On account of the New York Times' subscription policies, not all articles may be fully visible to all users.

Due to the New York Times' privacy restrictions, comments generated based on a user's display name cannot link back to the original article.

Use the stars in a comment box to rate that comment.

Comments may be rated from 0-4 stars.

Check the "Comments leaderboard" to view a summary of all the comments rated during this session.

Click the "Comment leaderboard" link in the bottom left corner of the footer to see a table of all the comments you've rated.

Each leaderboard table row includes a snippet of the comment text, the comment author (based on their New York Times display name), and your rating of that comment.

Sort the leaderboard alphabetically by author name or numerically by rating, ascending or descending, by clicking the arrows next to "Author" and "Rating" in the leaderboard table column headers.
